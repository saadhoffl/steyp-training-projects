Plan Your Day

At Wonderla, we want you to have a hassle-free and enjoyable experience. Hey, adventurer here is what you should do before visiting Wonderla.

Book Tickets and Food in Advance

Wanna make the most of your amusement park trip? Then be smart and book your tickets and food in advance. You will save time, money, and most importantly, you will not have to ride the roller coaster hangry!

Download the Park Map

Want to conquer the Wonderla like a pro? Download the map, plan your attack, and ride the best attractions hassle-free! Wonderla Kochi, Wonderla Bengaluru and Wonderla Hyderabad.

Check Your Transportation

Plan your transportation to the park so you don't get lost or stuck in traffic. Get direction to Wonderla Kochi, Wonderla Bengaluru and Wonderla Hyderabad.

Wear Comfortable Clothes

Choose comfy clothes within the prescribed dress code that won't leave you looking like a sweaty mess, and dress appropriately for the weather. It's always important to strike the perfect balance between comfort, style, and meeting the dress code requirements. Know more.

Pack Everything You Need

a hat or sunscreen to keep you from burning up, clothes that can handle all the wild rides, a towel, undergarments (yep, those too!), and a camera or phone to snap some pics and make everyone jealous of your adventures.

Arrive on Time

Don't miss out on the fun at the amusement park! Be sure to arrive early. All our parks open at 11 AM, but you can come as early as 9 AM, have breakfast, take selfies and prepare for an awesome day.
